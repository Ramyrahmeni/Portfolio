WaterCare Website

Full Stack Website / 18 Sep. 2018
View Github Repository

WaterCare is a captivating full stack website designed to inspire water donations, achieved through a harmonious integration of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP technologies. The website boasts a visually appealing interface, meticulously crafted using HTML and CSS to create a visually stunning and user-friendly design. Engaging content is brought to life with JavaScript, enhancing user interactivity and providing a dynamic browsing experience. The heart of WaterCare's functionality lies in its seamless donation process, powered by PHP. Users can contribute to the cause effortlessly by filling out a secure form, which utilizes PHP to process and securely transmit the donation information. This ensures that their support directly impacts water conservation efforts, as their contributions are efficiently managed and directed towards meaningful projects. Incorporating these technologies allows WaterCare to not only captivate visitors with its aesthetics and engaging content but also provide a smooth and secure platform for supporting the essential cause of water conservation.

Remote Banking Application

Full stack Webaite / 18 Sep. 2017
View Github Repository

During my internship at Amen Bank, I had the incredible opportunity to develop a cutting-edge online banking application, and I'm thrilled to share my journey! As a solo developer, I embraced the power of version control with Git, allowing me to efficiently manage and track changes throughout the development process. This enabled me to maintain a clean and organized codebase, collaborate seamlessly, and ensure the application's stability and reliability. Using a full stack approach, I harnessed the potential of HTML, Sass, and JavaScript to create a responsive website accessible across all platforms. Sass provided me with the flexibility to use variables, nested rules, and mixins, making my stylesheets more maintainable and organized. The user interface was carefully designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, ensuring a seamless banking experience for customers. One of the highlights of this project was the incorporation of Chart.js, which empowered users to visualize their financial data and budgets through dynamic and interactive charts. This feature enabled users to gain valuable insights and make informed financial decisions with ease. To enhance user convenience, I implemented both normal and dark mode options, catering to diverse user preferences and enhancing accessibility. Furthermore, in the backend, I leveraged the Django framework to model and manage the database effectively. Django played a crucial role in streamlining the backend processes and ensuring a robust and secure application.

Education

Engineering

National School Of Computer Science

Established in 1984, ENSI (National School for Computer Science) was the first Tunisian college specialised in IT. ENSI is a regional-class engineering college where teaching and research — with relevance to the practical world as a guiding principle — continue to be its primary purpose. The mission of ENSI is to advance knowledge and to educate students in science, technology, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve Tunisia in the 21st century. Degrees earned at ENSI include Master of Engineering, Master of Science, Master of Research and Doctorates.
